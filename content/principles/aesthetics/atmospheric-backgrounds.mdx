## Why This Matters

Flat, solid backgrounds are the absence of design. They're not minimalist—
they're incomplete. Even the most minimal interfaces benefit from atmospheric
depth: subtle gradients, grain, or texture that gives content a place to live.

The goal isn't decoration—it's creating an environment where your UI feels
grounded rather than floating in a void.

## Best Practices

- Layer multiple `radial-gradient` for organic gradient meshes
- Add subtle noise texture (2-5% opacity) for tactile quality
- Use CSS `backdrop-filter` for frosted glass effects
- Consider `mix-blend-mode` for layered transparency
- Keep atmospheric effects subtle—enhance, don't distract
