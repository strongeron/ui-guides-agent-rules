<Callout type="quote" title="From the Guidelines">
  Compositor-friendly. Prioritize GPU-accelerated properties (transform, opacity) & avoid properties that trigger reflows/repaints (width, height, top, left).
</Callout>

## Why This Matters

Animating properties like width, height, top, or left causes the browser to recalculate layout and repaint, which is slow. Transform and opacity can be handled by the GPU compositor, making animations smooth at 60fps or higher.
