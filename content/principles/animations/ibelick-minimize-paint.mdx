<Callout type="quote" title="UI Skills Rule">
  Minimize animating colors, backgrounds, and box-shadows. These trigger paint operations which are more expensive than compositor-only animations.
</Callout>

## Why This Matters

Paint operations require the browser to fill in pixels, which is CPU-intensive. While not as expensive as layout, animating paint-triggering properties like `background-color`, `color`, or `box-shadow` can still cause frame drops. For color transitions, use opacity on overlaid elements instead.
