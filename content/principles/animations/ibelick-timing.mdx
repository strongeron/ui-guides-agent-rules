<Callout type="quote" title="UI Skills Rule">
  Use ease-out for entrances (elements appear fast, settle slow). Keep interaction feedback under 200ms to feel instant.
</Callout>

## Why This Matters

Animation timing affects perceived performance:
- **ease-out** for entrances: Elements appear quickly and decelerate naturally
- **ease-in** for exits: Elements accelerate out of view
- **< 200ms** for interactions: Users perceive delays over 200ms as sluggish
