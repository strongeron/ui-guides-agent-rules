<Callout type="quote" title="From the Guidelines">
  Design forgiving interactions. Controls minimize finickiness with generous hit targets, clear affordances, & predictable interactions, e.g., prediction cones.
</Callout>

## Why This Matters

Make interactive elements easy to use by providing generous hit targets, clear visual feedback, and forgiving interaction patterns. Prediction cones in drag-and-drop interfaces help users maintain their dragging action even if the cursor drifts slightly.
