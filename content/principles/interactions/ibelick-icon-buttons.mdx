<Callout type="quote" title="UI Skills Rule">
  MUST add aria-label to icon-only buttons. Screen readers need text to announce the button's purpose.
</Callout>

## Why This Matters

Icon-only buttons have no visible text, so screen readers have nothing to announce. Users hear "button" with no context about what the button does. Adding `aria-label` gives screen readers text to announce.
