<Callout type="quote" title="UI Skills Rule">
  Use skeleton screens instead of spinners for loading states. Skeletons reduce perceived load time and prevent layout shift.
</Callout>

## Why This Matters

Skeleton screens:
- Show the structure of content before it loads
- Reduce perceived loading time
- Prevent cumulative layout shift (CLS)
- Feel faster than generic spinners

Spinners give no indication of what's coming and feel like waiting.
