<Callout type="quote" title="UI Skills Rule">
  NEVER mix primitive systems (e.g., Radix + Headless UI). Pick one and stick with it for consistency.
</Callout>

## Why This Matters

Mixing primitive libraries creates:
- Inconsistent keyboard navigation patterns
- Different focus behaviors
- Conflicting styling approaches
- Larger bundle sizes
- Confusing developer experience

Choose one primitive library and use it consistently throughout your project.
