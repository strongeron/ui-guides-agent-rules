<Callout type="quote" title="UI Skills Rule">
  MUST use AlertDialog for destructive actions. Users need explicit confirmation before irreversible operations like deletion.
</Callout>

## Why This Matters

Destructive actions like deleting data, canceling subscriptions, or removing accounts are irreversible. A simple click shouldn't trigger them. AlertDialog:
- Requires explicit action (not just clicking away)
- Focuses on the confirm/cancel buttons
- Makes the severity clear through visual design
