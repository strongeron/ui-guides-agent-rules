<Callout type="quote" title="UI Skills Rule">
  MUST use accessible primitives (Radix, Headless UI, Base UI) for keyboard navigation, focus trapping, and ARIA attributes.
</Callout>

## Why This Matters

Building accessible components from scratch is incredibly difficult. You need to handle:
- Keyboard navigation (arrow keys, Tab, Escape, Enter)
- Focus trapping in modals and dialogs
- ARIA attributes and live regions
- Screen reader announcements
- Focus restoration

Accessible primitive libraries handle all of this correctly out of the box.
