<Callout type="quote" title="From the Guidelines">
  Clear focus. Every focusable element shows a visible focus ring. Prefer :focus-visible over :focus to avoid distracting pointer users.
</Callout>

## Why This Matters

Focus indicators are essential for keyboard navigation. Use :focus-visible to show focus rings only during keyboard navigation, not when clicking with a mouse. The focus ring should have sufficient contrast and be clearly visible against all backgrounds.
