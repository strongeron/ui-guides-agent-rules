<Callout type="quote" title="From the Guidelines">
  No dead ends. Every screen offers a next step or recovery path.
</Callout>

## Why This Matters

Empty states, error pages, and completion screens should always provide clear next actions. Never leave users stuck wondering what to do. Offer suggestions, links to help, or ways to start over.
