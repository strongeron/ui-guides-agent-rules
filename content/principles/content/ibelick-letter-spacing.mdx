<Callout type="quote" title="UI Skills Rule">
  Avoid modifying letter-spacing unless explicitly requested. Type designers carefully craft letter spacing for readability.
</Callout>

## Why This Matters

Professional typefaces are meticulously designed with optimal letter spacing. Modifying it:
- Reduces readability
- Breaks the font's visual rhythm
- Often looks unprofessional

If you need wider or tighter text, consider a different font weight or font family instead.
