  Accessible content. Set accurate names (aria-label), hide decoration (aria-hidden) & verify in the accessibility tree.
</Callout>

## Why This Matters

Assistive technologies rely on proper ARIA attributes. Give interactive elements descriptive names with aria-label. Hide purely decorative elements with aria-hidden="true". Always test in the accessibility tree to ensure screen readers get meaningful information.
