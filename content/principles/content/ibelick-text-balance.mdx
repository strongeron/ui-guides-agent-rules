<Callout type="quote" title="UI Skills Rule">
  Use text-balance for headings and text-pretty for body text. These CSS properties improve line breaks and readability.
</Callout>

## Why This Matters

- **text-balance**: Balances line lengths in headings so no line is too short or long
- **text-pretty**: Prevents orphans (single words on the last line) in paragraphs

Both improve readability and visual appearance without manual line break management.
