<Callout type="quote" title="UI Skills Rule">
  Apply truncate or line-clamp for text in cards, lists, and dense layouts. Predictable overflow prevents layout breaking.
</Callout>

## Why This Matters

User-generated content can be any length. Without overflow handling:
- Cards expand unexpectedly
- Grids break alignment
- Text overlaps other elements

Use `truncate` for single lines, `line-clamp-*` for multi-line limits.
