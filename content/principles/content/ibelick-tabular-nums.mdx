<Callout type="quote" title="UI Skills Rule">
  MUST use tabular-nums (font-variant-numeric: tabular-nums) for data tables, prices, and numerical lists.
</Callout>

## Why This Matters

Proportional numbers have varying widths (1 is narrower than 8), causing columns to misalign. Tabular numbers have uniform widths, ensuring:
- Perfect column alignment
- Numbers don't jump when values change
- Professional, polished appearance
