<Callout type="quote" title="From the Guidelines">
  Non-breaking spaces for glued terms. Use a non-breaking space &nbsp; to keep units, shortcuts & names together: 10 MB → 10&nbsp;MB, ⌘ + K → ⌘&nbsp;+&nbsp;K, Vercel SDK → Vercel&nbsp;SDK.
</Callout>

## Why This Matters

Certain text elements should never wrap onto separate lines. Use &nbsp; (non-breaking space) in HTML or \\u00A0 in JavaScript to keep numbers with their units, keyboard shortcuts together, and brand names intact.
