  Stable skeletons. Skeletons mirror final content exactly to avoid layout shift.
</Callout>

## Why This Matters

Loading skeletons should match the dimensions and layout of the actual content. This prevents cumulative layout shift (CLS) when content loads. If content varies significantly, use the most common dimensions or a safe maximum.
