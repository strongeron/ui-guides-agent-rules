  MUST respect safe-area-inset for content near screen edges. Use pb-safe, pt-safe, or env(safe-area-inset-*) to avoid notches and home indicators.
</Callout>

## Why This Matters

Modern phones have notches, rounded corners, and home indicators that can obscure content. The `safe-area-inset` CSS environment variables let you add padding that respects these hardware features.
