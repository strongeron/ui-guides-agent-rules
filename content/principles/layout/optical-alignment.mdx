## Why This Matters

Mathematical centering doesn't always look centered to the human eye. Our perception is influenced by the visual weight and shape of elements. A play button, for example, appears off-center when mathematically centered because the triangle has more visual weight on the left.

Icons, triangles, and text often need small adjustments (1-2px) to appear properly aligned. Trust your eye over the numbers, but keep adjustments minimal.
