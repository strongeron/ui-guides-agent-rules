<Callout type="quote" title="Tailwind Golden Rule">
  Write mobile styles first, then use sm:, md:, lg: breakpoints to enhance for larger screens. Tailwind's breakpoints are min-width based.
</Callout>

## Why This Matters

Tailwind breakpoints are mobile-first (min-width). `md:flex` means "flex at medium screens AND UP." Starting desktop-first leads to verbose code with unnecessary overrides. Mobile-first is simpler and produces smaller CSS.

## Implementation

<CodeBlock title="Tailwind Classes" language="html">
{`<!-- Bad: Desktop-first (fighting the system) -->
<div class="flex flex-row md:flex-col sm:flex-col">

<!-- Good: Mobile-first (with the system) -->
<div class="flex flex-col md:flex-row">

<!-- Mobile: stacked (default)
     md+: side by side -->`}
</CodeBlock>
