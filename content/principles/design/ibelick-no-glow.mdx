<Callout type="quote" title="UI Skills Rule">
  NEVER use glow as primary affordance. Glows are decorative, not functional - they don't clearly indicate interactivity.
</Callout>

## Why This Matters

Glow effects:
- Are hard to see in bright environments
- Don't provide clear affordance (what can I click?)
- Look inconsistent across displays
- Can be expensive to render (blur + blend modes)

Use clear visual hierarchy, borders, and contrast instead.
