<Callout type="quote" title="Design System Principle">
  Consistent spacing creates visual rhythm and hierarchy. Inconsistent spacing makes interfaces feel unpolished and harder to scan. Use a spacing scale and apply it systematically.
</Callout>

## Why This Matters

Spacing guides the eye and creates visual relationships. When similar elements have different spacing, users lose their ability to quickly scan and group related content. A consistent spacing scale (4px, 8px, 12px, 16px, 24px, 32px) creates harmony and predictability.

## Implementation

<CodeBlock title="CSS/Tailwind" language="html">
{`<!-- Use consistent spacing scale -->
<div className="space-y-4">  <!-- 16px between all items -->
  <Card />
  <Card />
  <Card />
</div>

<!-- Component sections with consistent gaps -->
<section className="space-y-6">  <!-- 24px between sections -->
  <h2>Title</h2>
  <div className="space-y-3">  <!-- 12px between items -->
    <Item />
    <Item />
  </div>
</section>

<!-- Use Tailwind's spacing scale consistently -->
<!-- gap-2 (8px), gap-3 (12px), gap-4 (16px), gap-6 (24px) -->`}
</CodeBlock>
