<Callout type="quote" title="UI Skills Rule">
  Stick with Tailwind default shadows (shadow-sm, shadow, shadow-md, shadow-lg). Custom shadows create inconsistency.
</Callout>

## Why This Matters

Tailwind's shadow scale is carefully designed for:
- Visual consistency across your app
- Proper elevation hierarchy
- Good performance (no custom blur values)
- Easy maintenance

Custom shadows like `shadow-[0_10px_40px_rgba(0,0,0,0.3)]` create inconsistency and are hard to maintain.
