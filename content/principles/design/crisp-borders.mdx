  Crisp borders. Combine borders & shadows; semi-transparent borders improve edge clarity.
</Callout>

## Why This Matters

Shadows alone can look muddy and undefined. A subtle semi-transparent border makes edges crisp and clear. The key is using low opacity (8-12%) so the border adapts to both light and dark backgrounds.

## Implementation

<CodeBlock title="Tailwind CSS" language="html">
{`<!-- Bad: Shadow only - edges look soft/muddy -->
<div class="bg-card rounded-lg p-4 shadow-md">
  Content
</div>

<!-- Good: Shadow + semi-transparent border -->
<div class="bg-card rounded-lg p-4 shadow-md border border-black/[0.08] dark:border-white/[0.08]">
  Content
</div>

<!-- Alternative: Use design system border token -->
<div class="bg-card rounded-lg p-4 shadow-md border border-border">
  Content
</div>`}
</CodeBlock>
