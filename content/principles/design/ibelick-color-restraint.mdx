<Callout type="quote" title="UI Skills Rule">
  Limit accent colors to one per view. Multiple bright colors compete for attention and create visual chaos.
</Callout>

## Why This Matters

When everything is highlighted, nothing is. Multiple accent colors:
- Dilute visual hierarchy
- Make it unclear what's important
- Create cognitive overload
- Look unprofessional

Pick one accent color for primary actions and use neutrals for everything else.
