<Callout type="quote" title="UI Skills Rule">
  No purple or multicolor gradients. These are telltale signs of AI-generated or generic SaaS design.
</Callout>

## Why This Matters

Purple-to-pink and rainbow gradients have become the default output of AI design tools. They signal "generic" rather than "thoughtful design." If you must use gradients:
- Use brand colors
- Keep it subtle (single color family)
- Ensure sufficient contrast
