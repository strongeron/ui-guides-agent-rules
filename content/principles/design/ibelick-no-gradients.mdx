<Callout type="quote" title="UI Skills Rule">
  Avoid gradients unless explicitly requested. Solid colors are cleaner, more accessible, and easier to maintain.
</Callout>

## Why This Matters

Gradients often signal "AI-generated design" and can:
- Reduce contrast for text readability
- Create visual noise that distracts from content
- Be harder to maintain across light/dark themes
- Look dated quickly as design trends change

Solid colors are timeless and professional.
