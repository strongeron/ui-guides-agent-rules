<Callout type="quote" title="UI Skills Rule">
  MUST give empty states one clear action. Users need guidance on what to do next - don't leave them stranded.
</Callout>

## Why This Matters

Empty states are critical onboarding moments. Users seeing "No items" with no guidance will:
- Feel confused about what the app does
- Not know how to proceed
- Possibly abandon the product

Every empty state should explain what goes here and provide a clear next step.
