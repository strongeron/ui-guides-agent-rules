  Textarea behavior. In <textarea>, ⌘/⌃+Enter submits; Enter inserts a new line.
</Callout>

## Why This Matters

Textareas are for multi-line input, so Enter must create new lines. Users who want to submit can use the keyboard modifier (Cmd on Mac, Ctrl on Windows/Linux) plus Enter. This prevents accidental submission when writing paragraphs.
