<Callout type="quote" title="UI Skills Rule">
  MUST use cn utility (clsx + tailwind-merge) for class name merging. It handles conflicts and conditionals correctly.
</Callout>

## Why This Matters

When merging Tailwind classes, conflicts can occur (e.g., `p-4` and `p-2` both present). The cn() utility combines clsx for conditional logic with tailwind-merge for intelligent conflict resolution. Without it, you get duplicate or conflicting classes that lead to unpredictable styling.
