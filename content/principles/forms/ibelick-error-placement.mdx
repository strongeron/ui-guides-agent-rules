<Callout type="quote" title="UI Skills Rule">
  MUST show form errors next to the submit action. Users focus on the button when submitting - that's where they need to see errors.
</Callout>

## Why This Matters

When users submit a form, their attention is on the submit button. If errors only appear at individual fields (which may be scrolled out of view), users may not notice them. Showing a summary near the action ensures visibility.
